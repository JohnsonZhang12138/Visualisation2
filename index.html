Chocolate Bars in the World

Chocolate is one of the most popular candies in the world. This visualisation introduces information about the chocolate regional origin, percentage of cocoa, chocolate rating, chocolate tastes, ingredients and where the beans were grown.

Companies and Beans

The maps demonstrate the location of Chocolate companies and chocolate beans are grown. We can observe that most chocolate beans are grown in the north of South American.

The reason is that the United States of American has a strong demand for Chocolate.

One of the pieces of evidence is the United States of American has the most number of chocolate companies.

Also, chocolate beans have better growth in the north of South American.

Cocoa Percentage

The cocoa percentage of chocolate bars shifting from 2006 to 2020. In some time regions, people prefer 100% cocoa in chocolate bars.

Rating & Ingredients

In this visualisation, we can observe how types of ingredients affect the rating of chocolate bars.

The chocolate bars with 2 or 3 ingredients are more likely to have a high rating.

Tastes

This visualisation demonstrates the top 10 most common tastes of chocolate bars.

The top 2 tastes of chocolate bars are nutty and sweet.

In this case, we can predict that chocolate bars with nutty and sweet tastes are more popular.

This visualisation is created by Xi Zhang. The datasource is Chocolate Bar 2020.
